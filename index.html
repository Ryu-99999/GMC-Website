G Mining Group

  • G Mining Services ("GMS") — The technical and human capital core of the group. Founded in 2006 by Louis Gignac, a 2016 Canadian Mining Hall of Fame inductee. A multidisciplinary consultancy covering the full mining project lifecycle from greenfield to operations. gmining.com ↗

  • Other entities — Including Vardev Development Group, spanning gold and aggregate projects.

The Sumitomo Business Spirit
"Enriching lives and the world" — We never pursue easy gains. Integrity and strong unity are cherished principles from which we work to create value for society.

United across regions, cultures, and generations — Sumitomo cultivates opportunity, bridges possibilities, and creates a better tomorrow through passion, determination, and enduring values.

Sumitomo Corporation is one of Japan's leading sōgō shōsha — integrated trading and investment enterprises with a heritage rooted in the Sumitomo Business Spirit. With offices and operations spanning more than 130 countries, Sumitomo combines physical asset ownership with marketing, supply chain and financial capabilities across a broad range of industries.

In mining, Sumitomo takes a long-term approach — acquiring minority stakes with a view to building enduring partnerships with majority operators. The partnership with GMC reflects Sumitomo's commitment to expanding its mining investment footprint through a dedicated, operationally-focused vehicle.
